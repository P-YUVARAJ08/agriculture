Ploughing

I assume you are asking about "ploughing," which is the process of using a plow (a farming implement) to till and prepare the soil for planting crops.

Planting crops.

Planting crops is the agricultural practice of placing seeds or young plants into the soil with the aim of growing them into mature, harvestable crops

Fertilizer

Soil Testing: Start by conducting a soil test to determine its nutrient composition and pH levels. This will help you identify any nutrient deficiencies and pH imbalances that need to be corrected with fertilizer.

Crop yields

Yield in agriculture refers to the amount of a specific crop or agricultural product that is harvested from a given area of land or a single plant.

Harvest...

Harvesting is primarily done to gather agricultural or forest products for human consumption, animal feed, industrial use, or other applications.

Storage.

Depending on the type of crop, storage can take place in various facilities, such as silos, barns, warehouses, granaries, or specialized storage structures.
